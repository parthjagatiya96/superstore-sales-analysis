# Superstore Sales Dashboard (Tableau)

![Dashboard Preview](superstore_sales_dashboard_ss.PNG)

## Overview
This project is an interactive Tableau dashboard built using the Superstore dataset. It focuses on analyzing sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior across different regions in the US.

The goal of this dashboard is to make it easier to understand business performance and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Objective
- Track key metrics such as sales, profit, profit ratio, and orders  
- Analyze performance across regions, categories, sub-categories, and customer segments  
- Identify high-performing and underperforming areas  
- Enable easy data exploration through interactive filters  

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: ₹2.30M  
- Profit Margin: 12.47%  
- Return Rate: 5.91%  

---

## Dashboard Features
- Category and sub-category level analysis  
- Regional sales distribution (map view)  
- Segment-wise breakdown  
- Sales trend over time  
- Top 5 products by sales  
- Filters for region, year, and metric selection  

---

## Key Insights
- Sales show steady growth, but profit is not always consistent  
- The West region performs the best, while the Central region underperforms  
- Technology is the most profitable category  
- Furniture has lower margins, with some sub-categories generating losses  
- Consumer segment contributes the highest share of revenue  

---

## Recommendations
- Focus on improving profitability in the Furniture category  
- Review pricing and discount strategies for loss-making products  
- Apply successful strategies from high-performing regions  
- Identify and target high-value customers
